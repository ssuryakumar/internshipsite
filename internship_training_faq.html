1. What is Internship?

Internship is a methodology by which an individual, especially a student, is trained to inherit the organizational behaviour by constantly nurturing him/her with industrial best practices.

2. What documents do I bring to UNIQ for Internship?

Your college Identity Card.

3. When can I attend Internship?

Everyweek a new batch is started so the students can come and join in any day in the month of June or July according to their convenience.

4. How many students can join for this program?

There is no minimum and maximum limit to join the training.
Any number of individuals can participate even a single student can also participate in this training.

5. What is the training duration?

The duration of Internship is either 5 days (3 hours/day) or 10 days (3 hours/day) which is students’ choice.

6. Can I attend Internship for more than 10 Days?

Yes, students can very well attend more than 10 days also.

7. What are the certificates will be given?

Internship & Project completion Certificates are issued.

8. Will the certificates be useful for my placements?

Yes, ofcourse the professional certificates with ISO 9001:2008 certification awarded by us to the students are highly recognized by many recruiters. Since company expects a practical knowledge from the students, it’s obvious that UNIQ student will always have unique identity for the interview.

9. Will you provide training materials after my training?

Yes, we will provide complete training material on all the topics. Separate CDs with all the materials will be given to all the participants.

10. Will you guide students for placement?

Yes definitely, since the trainers at UNIQ have vast MNC industrial experience, we will definitely guide for your placements. Placement training (Aptitude, GD, HR Interview) is also provided to all our participants.
